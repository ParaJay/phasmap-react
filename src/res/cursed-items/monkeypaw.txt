Grants a wish for those who ask it.

Wishes can be found scattered inside Sunny Meadows and will be added to your Journal when found.
These are not required to use the paw but are just a permanent note to help you remember.

Number of wishes corresponds to the difficulty or reward multiplier
    0x - 1.9x Rewards: 5 Wishes
    2x - 2.9x Rewards: 4 Wishes
    3x or higher Rewards: 3 Wishes

Wishes:
    I wish to be sane
    I wish to be safe
    I wish to leave
    I wish to see the ghost
    I wish for activity
    I wish to trap the ghost
    I wish to revive my friend
    I wish for knowledge
    I wish for {weather}
